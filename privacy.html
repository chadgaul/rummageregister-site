Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Rummage Register.

We Collect No Personal Information Using Our Application

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in Rummage Register.

Individual settings relating to the Rummage Register app are not personal and are stored only on your device. We don’t process that information at all and have no access to it.

App Analytics

To help us better understand how people use the Rummage Register app and to continue improving upon it, we use analytics information. These services provide us with some generic usage, transaction, and other statistics that are completely anonymous and cannot personally identify you in any way.
